HOW TO PLAY

>

The counter indicates the number of remaining unflagged mines. Harder = more mines !

>

Click on a cell. The number you get indicates the amount of mines in the cells around it.

>

Try to guess where mines are, and right-click the cells to flag them.

>

If you revealed all the mine-free cells, congratulations : you WIN !

>

If you click on a mine... BOOM !
